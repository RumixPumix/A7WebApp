PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: May 09, 2025

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal Information

We collect:

  • Usernames
  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Uploaded files (visible to admins)

Automatically Collected Data

We use essential cookies for login sessions only. No tracking cookies are used.

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We process data to:

  • Provide game server hosting
  • Enable file sharing
  • Maintain forum functionality
  • Secure our platform

3. LEGAL BASIS (GDPR)

We process data under Performance of Contract (Article 6(1)(b)) as data is required to deliver our services.

4. DATA RETENTION

We retain data until account deletion. Deactivated accounts are purged within 30 days.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

EU/UK users may:

  • Request access to their data
  • Request deletion
  • Update account information

Requests will be processed within 30 days. Contact: privacy@ace7esports.com

6. SECURITY

We implement password hashing and regular security audits. However, as a private platform, users should maintain their own backups.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

Updates will be posted here with a new revision date.
